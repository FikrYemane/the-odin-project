Lasagna

Discription

Lasagne, or the singular lasagna, is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating
with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce) and other vegetables, cheese (which may include ricotta and parmesan),
and seasonings and spices such as garlic, oregano and basil.

Ingridents

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage

  • ¾ pound lean ground beef

  • ½ cup minced onion

  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

  2. preheat oven
  3. put sauses in loose lasagna
  4. cook
